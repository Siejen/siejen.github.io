About Siejen

Raised in the Big Apple and transplanted to the Golden State, Siejen is an empathetic and solutions oriented marketing and business professional who is seeking to connect with like-minded individuals who are interested in disruptive innovations and leveraging social media and technology to build bridges across global communities. Siejen's ultimate goal is to create sustainable impact and become a "Catalyst for Change."

In her oodles and oodles of free time, you'll find Siejen poring through a book; globe-trotting with her husband, Pete; canoodling with her wheaten terrier, Milo Brindley Stevenson, or reaching a state of nirvana through yoga.
